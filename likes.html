VIDEOS

Check out some of the YouTube channels and videos that I love!

Ashley's bestdressed YouTube channel is one of my favorites. Most of her videos have to do with fashion or home decor, but she makes a lot of videos about painting, thrifting, hauls, and more. She's really talented; she won the senior superlative for best dressed, which is a feat in and of itself, and her wardrobe and outfits show why. Also, she can do incredible things with a sewing machine. If I had her sense of style, a good source of my stress would vanish.

Jane ASMR is my favorite ASMR channel. I get that a lot of people get grossed out by the sound of people chewing, and honestly, me too. It's one of my pet peeves. But for some reason, it doesn't bother me when I listen to it. In fact, it really helps me focus when I'm doing homework. The video shown is the one I'm listening to as I type these words. Jane ASMR is the only one I listen to because she never talks in the video, only ever eats her food, so I can just add all her videos on my queue and not have to worry about eventually needing to stop what I'm doing to skip past the talking parts.

A final channel that I like to watch a lot is Vogue. They have a lot of interesting series, but my favorite ones are Diary of a Model, 73 Questions, and 24 Hours With. The video shown is part of the Diary of a Model series, and it follows top model Anok Yai around a busy day during New York Fashion Week. I like this video especially because it makes the models seem more human instead of this far-away, perfect object, and it really shows their personality.
